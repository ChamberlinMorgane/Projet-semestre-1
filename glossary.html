WOMEN'S POWER

The Seneca Falls Convention
This convention held in July 1848 was the first convention on the topic of women's rights in the United States.
Anarchism
Anarchism is a political thought that wants no more hierarchy in society, which means that there are no more leaders or controlled people. Anarchists want to be totally free and subject to no one.
Socialism
Socialism is a political trend and a way of thinking that wants a more equal society by eliminating inequalities. This current is democratized from 1820
Capitalism
Capitalism is a social organization that was very successful during the revolution. The industrial capital, the source of income, the means of production and exchange do not belong to those who use them through their own work
Jonh F Kennedy
He was the youngest President of the United States, entering the White House at only 43 years old in 1961. Unfortunately he was assassinated in 1963 after two years in office during a parade. He was shot and died of his wounds.
Supreme Court
It is the highest court in the United States. This court handles the most important cases and its judgments are not subject to appeal.
Intersectionality
This term refers to the situation of a person who suffers from several types of discrimination at the same time. This term was proposed by Kimberlé Williams Crenshaw in 1989. This term is mainly used to denounce the sexism and racism suffered by African-American women.
Gloden Globe
It is one of the most important film awards ceremonies in the United States. Every year they reward the best films, the best figures of cinema or American television.
United States Senate
It is part of the legislative branch of government. It is composed of 2 senators per state, so 100 in all, elected for 6 years.
