A PROPOS

My name is CHAMBERLIN Morgane, I am 18 years old and I am currently in my first year of a Bachelor of Technology (BUT) Multimedia and Internet Profession in Montbéliard. The website that is proposed here was created following an educational project on feminism given by the teachers of this BUT. This project serves to familiarize us with the world of the WEB and the creation of contents. I chose the theme of feminism in the United States because this country is a source of dreams so I wanted to explain its history of feminism. for the feminist, I chose her because she is very inspiring and she has a great influence. To finish the movie, I chose it because Wonder woman is one of the strongest and oldest heroines that was created. That's why I decided to put her on my site.
